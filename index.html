About us:

At VeggieMeals, we’re passionate about healthy eating and a healthier planet. We provide vegetarian, vegan, and dietary-friendly meals while helping people make informed nutrition choices. Our mission is to offer delicious, nutritious recipes and show how small changes in food choices can reduce carbon footprints. With VeggieMeals, eating well means living well—while protecting the Earth. Click this link to find out more about us.

Our Goal

By 2027, we aim to have 5,000 active users. If each user replaces their meat consumption with VeggieMeals or Quorn meat alternatives, we will save approximately 1347.2 KG of CO2 emissions per user. This significant change will lead to a combined total reduction of 6,735,823.5 KG of CO2 emissions, showcasing our commitment to a more sustainable and eco-friendly future.
