Halloumi Burger Recipe

Seasoned to Perfection

Benefits

Halloumi burgers are a delightful option for vegetarians and cheese lovers alike, offering a satisfying texture and rich flavor. Halloumi is high in protein, making it a great choice for those looking to maintain a balanced diet without meat. It's also an excellent source of calcium, which supports strong bones and teeth. The grilling or frying process adds a lovely golden crust to the cheese while maintaining its unique, chewy consistency—perfect for creating a filling and nutritious meal.

Description

Halloumi burgers are a delightful option for vegetarians and cheese lovers alike, offering a satisfying texture and rich flavor. Halloumi is high in protein, making it a great choice for those looking to maintain a balanced diet without meat. It's also an excellent source of calcium, which supports strong bones and teeth. The grilling or frying process adds a lovely golden crust to the cheese while maintaining its unique, chewy consistency—perfect for creating a filling and nutritious meal.

Recipe -

  1. Use a food processor or hand blender to puree every ingredient other than the black cumin (this makes the sauce).
  2. Preheat the Tepan grill to 410F (210C) or level 8. Oil the pan with how much you think is necessary
    • Use one zone to fry the halloumi on both sides until golden brown, then remove from the tepan grill or pan.
    • In the other zone, briefly toast the burger buns cut-side down.
  3. Build the burger -
    • Start with the bottom half of the burger bun
    • Add a layer of sauce followed by lettuce leaves,
    • Halloumi, and
    • More sauce.
    • Finally, close the burger with the top half of the bun, and serve while still hot

necessary ingredients and equiment -

  • 1 piece of halloumi (split longways)
  • Bread Buns (for burgers)
  • Lettuce
