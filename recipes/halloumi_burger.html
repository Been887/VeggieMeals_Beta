Recipe -

  1. Use a food processor or hand blender to puree every ingredient other than the black cumin (this makes the sauce).
  2. Preheat the Tepan grill to 410F (210C) or level 8. Oil the pan with how much you think is necessary
    • Use one zone to fry the halloumi on both sides until golden brown, then remove from the tepan grill or pan.
    • In the other zone, briefly toast the burger buns cut-side down.
