Avocado On Toast

A high protein snack

Benefits

Avocado on toast is not just a trendy breakfast choice, but also a healthy one. Packed with monounsaturated fats, avocados are heart-friendly and can help reduce bad cholesterol levels. They're also rich in fiber, which aids digestion, and contain a variety of vitamins and minerals like potassium, vitamin E, and vitamin C. When paired with whole grain toast, this dish provides a balanced meal that can keep you feeling full and energized throughout the day. Additionally, avocados are known for their anti-inflammatory properties and can contribute to better skin health.

Description

Picture this: a perfectly ripe avocado, sliced or mashed, and spread generously over a slice of warm, crunchy toast. The vibrant green contrasts beautifully with the earthy brown of the toast. To elevate the flavors, you can add a sprinkle of sea salt, a dash of red pepper flakes, or a squeeze of fresh lemon juice. Some even like to top it with a poached egg or a handful of cherry tomatoes for an extra layer of taste and nutrition. It's a simple yet delightful dish that can be customized to suit your taste preferences. Enjoy!

Recipe -

  1. Toast 2 (or more) slices of bread

  2. While bread is toasting -
    • Combine avocado flesh and apple cider vinegar in small bowl.
    • Mash with potato masher until mostly smooth.
    • Season with salt and pepper.

  3. In another bowl, mash chickpeas (with hands or fork) until flaky -
    Season with salt and pepper.

  4. Spread the mashed avocado onto the toast slices and top with the smashed chickpeas.

  5. Decorate with sprouts, microgreens or radish slices and enjoy!

necessary ingredients and equiment -

  • toast slice X2
  • small avocado/half of big avocado
  • chickpeas
  • "microgreens",
  • potato masher/fork
  • bowl X2
